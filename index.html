About Kalystia Therapy

Wherever you are, you can receive IV treatments thanks to Kalystia Therapy, a medical spa. For both individuals and families, we offer treatments that are bundled with both aesthetic and health benefits. All procedures are carried out by licensed medical professionals with a wealth of knowledge in both the medical and aesthetic fields.

About Us

IV Treatments

Our intravenous therapy supplies vital nutrients and fluids that the body requires for optimum performance. directly into your bloodstream. This ensures prompt action, whether it be to improve mood, skin attractiveness, energy levels, hangover, or inflammation.

Flexibility

We are aware that getting to your preferred medical spa for your treatment may be challenging for a variety of reasons. We therefore set out to ensure that you enjoy the elegance and serenity of a medical spa from the comfort of your own home or workplace.

Professionals

GLUTATHIONE INFUSION

We provide glutathione supplements, which are an amino acid that is naturally generated by the liver. Its advantages include tissue construction and repair, the generation of chemicals and proteins necessary by the body, and immune system activity. It also helps with aging, alcoholism, liver illness, heart disease, and a variety of other ailments.

VITAMIN C

Injectable vitamins C is easier and faster to absorb since it bypass the digestive system and enter the circulation directly. It aids in improving energy levels while feeling "run down," fighting depression, losing weight through increasing metabolism, and relieving exhaustion and lethargy. Creates a strong immune system reducing cancer risk, improves skin quality and appearance leaving you with a young appearance and more.

COLLAGEN

Collagen is a protein that is present in connective tissue such as skin, bones, tendons, and cartilage. Heavy sun exposure reduces collagen formation, smoking and excessive alcohol use can hasten the loss process resulting to signs of aging, such as dry, sagging skin. Our collagen supplements helps in Increasing bone density, Maintenance of joints, ligaments, and skin, conceal scars and big pores.

IRON INFUSION

Are you fatigued, dizzy, and suffering from persistent headaches? When your natural iron stores are low, iron infusion is one approach to get more iron, giving you more energy, easier breathing, better sleep quality, mood, and memory.
